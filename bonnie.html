Who is Bonnie?

Bonnie is well known to be one of the scariest FNAF characters. His vibe is just sort of off in a way that the other characters cannot quite relate to. He is freky, funky, and a bunny! I personally really like Bonnie, and I think he is definitely work a second look! Especially some of his variants. They really show how creative you can get with animatronic design!
