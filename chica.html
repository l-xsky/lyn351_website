Who is Chica?

It has been a joke online to call her Chica the kitchen, but she is, in reality, a chicken. She is the girl of the group. She is well known for her love of food! She loves pizza, she loves singing, and she carries a cute little cupcake with her everywhere she goes. She is my favorite!
