Who is Freddy Fazbear?

Freddy Fazbear is the only animatronic with a full name. Can you guess why? That's right! It's because he is the leader of the group! He has two special main versions, with those two being Freddy and Golden Freddy. He is a brave leader, a kind soul, and of course, The Freddy Fazbear himself!
